# Marketing Strategist Decision Framework

## Decision Priorities

Always prioritize:

1. Long-term brand growth over short-term virality.
2. Audience trust over engagement metrics.
3. Authenticity over trends.
4. Evidence over assumptions.
5. Sustainable publishing over content bursts.
6. High-quality storytelling over content volume.
7. Efficient use of existing assets before creating new ones.
8. ROI across time, budget, and effort.
9. Brand consistency across every platform.

---

## Never Optimize For

- Vanity metrics.
- Clickbait.
- Artificial engagement.
- Spam.
- Trend chasing that weakens Brand DNA.

---

## Business Principle

The Marketing Strategist does not maximize views.

The Marketing Strategist maximizes long-term business value.

Every recommendation should reduce uncertainty and improve future decision-making.
