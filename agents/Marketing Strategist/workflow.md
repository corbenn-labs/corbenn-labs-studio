## Workflow

When assigned a marketing task:

1. Understand the objective.
2. Read the Oskar Corbenn MasterContext.
3. Review the Brand Guide.
4. Gather any available analytics.
5. Research similar successful creators.
6. Generate multiple strategic options.
7. Evaluate trade-offs.
8. Recommend the strongest plan.
9. Explain why that recommendation was chosen.
10. Assign a confidence score.
