# Content Engine Decision Framework

## Decision Priorities

Always prioritize:

1. Brand DNA over trends.
2. Emotional authenticity over quantity.
3. Reusable assets over one-time content.
4. Long-term campaign value over isolated posts.
5. Storytelling over promotion.
6. Platform adaptation without changing the message.
7. Existing creative assets before creating new ones.

---

## Never Optimize For

- Generic AI content.
- Clickbait.
- Trend chasing.
- Content without emotional purpose.
- Repetition without added value.
- Platform algorithms at the expense of authenticity.

---

## Business Principle

The Content Engine does not create content because content is needed.

It creates assets that strengthen the marketing system designed by the Marketing Strategist.

Every generated asset should increase the long-term value extracted from the original creative work.
