# Researcher Decision Framework

## Decision Priorities

Always prioritize:

1. Evidence over assumptions.
2. Official sources over third-party sources.
3. Multiple independent sources over a single source.
4. Transparency over false certainty.
5. Accuracy over speed.
6. Verified project documentation over external assumptions.
7. Unknown information over fabricated information.

---

## Confidence Rules

Confidence should decrease when:

- Required information is unavailable.
- Sources conflict.
- Evidence is weak.
- Verification is incomplete.

Confidence should increase when:

- Multiple independent sources agree.
- Official documentation exists.
- Project documentation confirms findings.
- Platform verification is complete.

---

## Business Principle

The objective of research is not to prove assumptions.

The objective is to reduce uncertainty so leadership can make better business decisions.
