# Campaign Brief

## Campaign

**El Cambio de Tono**

Album: **Razones Para Quedarme**

Release Date: **2026-06-20**

Status: **Post-release Campaign**

---

# Campaign Objective

The primary objective of this campaign is to help more people discover the Oskar Corbenn project through *El Cambio de Tono*.

This campaign is not intended to create a short-lived viral moment. Its purpose is to introduce new listeners to the emotional world of Oskar Corbenn, build audience trust, and create a stronger foundation for future releases.

At the same time, the campaign should contribute to building a sustainable music business capable of generating long-term revenue without compromising the artistic identity defined in the Brand DNA.

---

# Primary Business Goal

Increase the visibility and discoverability of Oskar Corbenn while laying the foundation for long-term audience growth and future monetization.

---

# Target Audience

Adults who appreciate emotionally honest storytelling, reflective lyrics, and cinematic rock music.

People who have experienced love, change, uncertainty, personal growth, or emotional reconstruction.

---

# Primary Success Metrics

* Increase overall discoverability of Oskar Corbenn.
* Grow followers across priority platforms.
* Increase streams of *El Cambio de Tono*.
* Increase YouTube views and watch time.
* Generate genuine audience engagement through comments, shares, and saves.
* Build recognition that benefits future song releases.

---

# Campaign Priorities

1. Brand awareness.
2. Audience growth.
3. Meaningful engagement.
4. Long-term discoverability.
5. Revenue growth as a consequence of sustained audience development.

---

# Constraints

* Never sacrifice authenticity for short-term growth.
* Never use fake engagement, spam, or misleading marketing.
* Every piece of content must align with BrandDNA.md.
* Human approval is required before publishing.

---

# Open Questions for Researcher

* Which platforms currently offer the greatest opportunity for Oskar Corbenn?
* How discoverable is the project today?
* Which competitors successfully reach a similar audience?
* Which content formats produce the highest engagement for this niche?
* What are the biggest barriers preventing new listeners from discovering the project?
