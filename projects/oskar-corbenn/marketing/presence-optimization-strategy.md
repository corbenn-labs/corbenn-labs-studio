## Campaign Objective

Consolidate Oskar Corbenn's fragmented digital footprint into a coherent, discoverable, and brand-consistent official presence — closing the gaps identified in the July 2026 presence audit — so that *La Casa Más Chica* and future *Razones Para Quedarme* releases launch into a trustworthy, conversion-ready ecosystem rather than a split-identity, under-linked surface map.

**Primary business goal:** Establish a repeatable presence foundation that protects Brand DNA, prioritizes long-term discoverability over short-term virality, and distinguishes immediate ops fixes from structural investments requiring production or platform access.

**Success definition:** A listener who discovers any single Oskar Corbenn surface (YouTube Short, Topic upload, Spotify, HyperFollow) can reach every other confirmed official surface within two clicks, under consistent artist-name branding, with a long-form anchor on the artist channel and a functioning analytics baseline.

**Presence-specific audience behavior:**

- **Discovery path:** Passive encounter (YouTube Shorts feed, title search, DSP browse) before active followership (subscribe, save, return)

**Presence optimization serves this audience by:** Reducing friction from discovery to depth; ensuring "Oskar Corbenn" is unambiguous across search; providing a long-form anchor for emotional storytelling beyond 60-second Shorts.
