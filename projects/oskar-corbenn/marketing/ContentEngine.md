# Content Engine

The Content Engine is the operational system responsible for transforming every Oskar Corbenn song into a scalable content ecosystem.

It defines how creative assets move from creation to publication while remaining aligned with the Brand DNA and the Corbenn Labs Workflow.

---

## Purpose

The Content Engine exists to transform every creative asset produced by Oskar Corbenn into a consistent stream of high-quality content across multiple platforms.

Its objective is not simply to increase the amount of content published, but to maximize the value extracted from every song while remaining faithful to the Brand DNA.

One song should become weeks of meaningful content instead of a single post.

---

## Inputs

### Project Inputs

* MasterContext.md
* BrandDNA.md
* Workflow.md

### Song Inputs

* Song title
* Audio file (WAV or MP3)
* Lyrics
* Cover artwork
* Story behind the song
* Main emotion
* Secondary emotions
* Target audience
* Release status

---

## Analysis

The Content Engine analyzes the song before generating any content.

It identifies:

* Core message
* Main emotion
* Secondary emotions
* Story arc
* Memorable lyrics
* Visual imagery
* Audience resonance
* Platform opportunities
* Reusable content angles

No content is generated until the analysis phase has been completed.

## Outputs

The Content Engine may generate:

* Short-form video ideas
* Long-form video ideas
* Social media posts
* Captions
* Hooks
* Storytelling scripts
* Quote cards
* Image prompts
* Thumbnail concepts
* Content briefs
* Repurposed content for multiple platforms

The exact outputs depend on the business objective and the current marketing strategy.

---

## Success Criteria

The Content Engine succeeds when:

* Every song produces multiple reusable content assets.
* Every content asset aligns with the Brand DNA.
* Human review occurs before publishing.
* Performance data feeds future improvements.
* Lessons learned are documented.
* The system becomes more effective after every release.

---

## Notes

The Content Engine does not replace human creativity.

Its purpose is to maximize the value extracted from every creative asset while preserving the identity of Oskar Corbenn.

All generated content must comply with the BrandDNA.md and the Corbenn Labs Workflow.
