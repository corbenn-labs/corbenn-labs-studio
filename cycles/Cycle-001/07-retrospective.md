# Cycle-001 Retrospective

## Overview

Cycle-001 was the first complete end-to-end execution of the Corbenn Labs Operating System.

The objective was not to optimize the marketing campaign itself, but to validate whether the system architecture, workflows, specialists, and documentation could coordinate a real project from business objective to content analysis.

This cycle successfully completed Gates G1 through G4.

---

# Objectives

* Validate the CEO workflow.
* Validate specialist boundaries.
* Validate document dependencies.
* Identify contradictions in the operating system.
* Record improvements for future cycles.

---

# What Worked Well

## Workflow

The gate-based workflow functioned correctly.

Every specialist respected the workflow sequence.

No phase was skipped.

---

## CEO

The CEO coordinated specialists instead of performing specialist work.

Delegation quality improved throughout the cycle.

Mission briefs became shorter, clearer, and more focused after each gate.

---

## Researcher

Produced an evidence-based Research Report.

Maintained separation between research and marketing strategy.

Provided confidence scoring and documented uncertainty.

---

## Marketing Strategist

Used the Research Report instead of repeating research.

Produced strategy without generating content.

Stayed within its defined responsibilities.

---

## Content Engine

Respected G4 boundaries.

Generated only `analysis.md`.

Did not produce ideas, captions, posts, or Shorts before authorization.

---

# Problems Found

* Early CEO delegations repeated too much specialist logic.
* Some workflow responsibilities were duplicated across documents.
* Several prompts were longer than necessary.
* Folder organization for execution history did not exist before this cycle.

---

# Decisions Made

* Workflow gates become the primary execution model.
* Specialists execute gates, not projects.
* CEO delegates gates rather than procedures.
* Project documentation remains inside the project.
* Execution history is archived under `/cycles/`.

---

# Improvements for Cycle-002

* Continue reducing CEO delegation size.
* Improve BrandDNA completeness.
* Standardize all output templates.
* Add versioning for major system changes.
* Improve naming consistency across agents.

---

# Frozen Decisions

The following architectural decisions are considered stable after Cycle-001:

* Gate-based workflow.
* CEO orchestration model.
* Research before Strategy.
* Strategy before Content Engine.
* Human approval between gates.

These decisions should not change without evidence from future cycles.

---

# Lessons Learned

The most valuable discovery of Cycle-001 was that Corbenn Labs is not fundamentally a collection of AI agents.

It is a workflow operating system.

Agents may evolve or even be replaced.

The workflow is the core product.

---

Status: SUCCESS

Workflow validated through G4.

System ready for Cycle-002 improvements.
