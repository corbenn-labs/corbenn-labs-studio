# Corbenn Labs Architecture

## Purpose

This document describes the high-level architecture of Corbenn Labs and how information flows through the operating system.

The workflow is governed by the CEO, who coordinates specialized agents responsible for different phases of execution.

The architecture is intentionally modular so specialists can evolve independently without changing the overall workflow.

---

---

## Principles

- Every stage has a clear owner.
- Information flows forward.
- Decisions flow downward.
- Knowledge flows back to the CEO.
- Human approval controls workflow gates.
